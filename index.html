Headline News

Fed Governor Bowman Questions Rate Hike Efficacy Amid Inflation Spike

Fed Governor Michelle Bowman warned against raising rates in response to the current inflation surge — arguing past attempts driven by energy and tariffs have proven largely ineffective. This introduces notable dissent within the FOMC.

Why It Matters: Bowman's comments could temper expectations for aggressive hikes, easing pressure on bond yields and growth stocks.

Under the Radar

Dell Technologies surged 32% — its best day ever — after reporting a 757% year over year increase in server revenue, far exceeding analyst expectations.

Smart Money Watch

Institutional investors are rotating beyond high-flying hardware names into foundational technologies. Nvidia's multibillion dollar bet on photonics is drawing significant smart money attention as a long-term efficiency play for data center infrastructure.

Market Takeaway

Today's action reflects a battle between macro uncertainty and undeniable tech advancement. Positioning for both macro resilience and targeted tech exposure remains paramount.
