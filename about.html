Our story

TickerBrew exists because most financial news is designed to keep you reading — not to help you think. We built something different.

Why we built this

The noise was
unbearable.

Every morning, serious investors face the same problem: too much information, too little signal. Financial media is built around pageviews and engagement, not portfolio performance. You end up reading twelve articles about the same earnings miss and missing the one macro shift that actually matters.

TickerBrew was built to fix that. Every morning, before U.S. markets open, we deliver a single, focused brief that tells you exactly what moved overnight, what it means, and what to watch next. No recycled commentary. No filler. No noise.

We cover the stories institutional desks are paying attention to — Fed policy, macro cross currents, geopolitical shifts, tech market leadership, and smart money positioning — and we synthesize them into something you can actually act on in five minutes.

The daily brief is free to access — no credit card, no catch. And as we grow, we're building optional premium tiers for readers who want to go deeper. But the morning brief that started it all? That stays free.

What we stand for

The TickerBrew principles

Signal over noise
We read hundreds of sources so you don't have to. If a story doesn't carry real market weight, it doesn't make it into your inbox. Quality over quantity, every time.
Context, not just headlines
Every story comes with a "Why It Matters" — because knowing what happened is useless without understanding what it means for your positioning.
Respect your time
Five minutes. That's what we design every brief to take. If it can't be read in that window, we haven't done our job of cutting it down to what truly matters.
Free to start
The daily morning brief is free — no credit card, no trial countdown. We're also building optional paid tiers for readers who want an evening recap, weekly deep dives, and more. The core brief will always have a free tier.
No spam. Ever.
We will never sell or share your email address — period. You'll get the daily brief every weekday morning, plus the occasional update from us when something important changes (like a new product tier launching). Every email has a one-click unsubscribe. That's the full list of things we'll send you.
